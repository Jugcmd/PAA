 	Smart environment/ IoT integration (10%)	Inadequate or no understanding of IoT principles. The application’s potential use in a smart environment is ignored or misunderstood, with no relevant considerations addressed.
 	Limited understanding of IoT principles. Basic aspects are mentioned but not well understood or appreciated, leading to significant gaps in considering the application’s use in a smart environment.
 	Significant gaps in understanding of IoT principles. Some key aspects are identified but the consideration is superficial and lacks depth, potentially missing important factors for IoT integration.
 	Good understanding of IoT principles. Most key aspects are identified and considered, but the appreciation of the complexity and requirements for IoT integration is limited.	Very good understanding of IoT principles. Key aspects such as connectivity, data management, and security are well identified and considered, demonstrating a solid appreciation of the application’s use/potential use in a smart environment.
 	Excellent understanding of IoT principles. The consideration is thorough and detailed, covering all key aspects and demonstrating a strong appreciation of the complexity and requirements for use/potential use in a smart environment.	Outstanding understanding of IoT principles. The consideration is comprehensive and meticulously detailed, covering all key aspects and demonstrating an exceptional appreciation of the complexity and requirements of relevant smart environments  and integrating the application into these.
